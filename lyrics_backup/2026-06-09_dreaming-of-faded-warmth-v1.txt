[RAW LYRICS]
[Hook]
There is a fire burning low tonight
I am drawing closer to your gentle light
Got a feeling we should take it slow
Let the warmth of the embers glow

[Verse 1]
We took the long way down this dusty road
Left the heavy world and all its load
Now it is just the shadows and your smile
Tell me we can stay right here for a while

[Pre-Chorus]
I trace the lines upon your resting hand
You are the quiet peace I understand

Leaves me craving just a little more

[Chorus]
I am dreaming of that faded warmth we share
Tangled up inside the midnight air
Every single breath you take is mine
Lost together in the perfect time
